Who we are...

Our Vision

We seek to grow, expand and be innovative enough to deliver remarkable real leather products that satisfy today’s customer.


Our Mission

Our mission is to use real African pure leather to manufacture products that carries an international class.


Our Values

Good customer value. Real leather products that satisfy today’s customer. Success that is derived from continual testing and learning.


Our shoes are a hand made to custom and personalized specifications. Made to fit, made to last, made to match. We handcraft customized pure leather products, to suit our client’s business, taste & environment.

All our products are high quality leather products made from 100% genuine leather. The pure leather adds a distinct finish and classy appeal to our fine leather accessories, formals or semi-formals, style up with attire of your choice and it will just add a touch of pride to your whole attire.
